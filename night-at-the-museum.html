Night at the Museum

Digital Humanities Web Design Research
Digital Humanities Web Design Data Visualization Research Interactive

Night at the Museum is a digital humanities project that reimagines the museum experience through an interactive web interface. The work explores how cultural collections can be made more accessible, personal, and dynamic through digital curation and storytelling. Visitors navigate a virtual museum space after closing hours — encountering objects, stories, and connections that are often invisible during daytime visits.
