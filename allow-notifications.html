Allow Notifications

Game Design

Click here to Play

CONTROLS:

  • Player 1: AD to Move Left/Right
  • Player 2: FH to Move Left/Right
  • Player 1: W to Jump / S to Shield
  • Player 2: G to Jump / H to Shield
  • Avoid notifications and knock your opponent off the screen!

ABOUT:

Allow Notifications uses the language and conventions of smartphone notification interfaces as its primary mechanic. By appropriating familiar UI patterns — pop-ups, permission dialogs, alerts — the game questions the relationship between users and the apps that compete for their attention.
