Sincerely, Me

Game & Interactive Design

Click here to Play

CONTROLS:

  • Click to read letters
  • Make choices to shape the story

ABOUT:

Sincerely, Me is an interactive narrative experience told through the intimate medium of letters. Players inhabit the role of a correspondent, navigating relationships and emotions through the words they choose to write — and the words they choose to leave out.
