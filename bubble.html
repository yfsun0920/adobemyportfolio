Bubble

Watch Film

ABOUT:

Bubble is a motion and video project exploring the ephemeral nature of communication and connection. The piece uses the visual metaphor of bubbles — forming, floating, and popping — to explore how ideas and conversations expand and dissolve in the digital age.
