Wheels

3D Modeling Animation
3D Modeling Animation Maya Rendering Character Design

Wheels is a 3D modeling and animation project that explores movement, mobility, and form through a character or object centered on the motif of wheels. The project demonstrates technical proficiency in 3D modeling, rigging, and animation, while developing a distinct visual language through texture, lighting, and rendering choices. The animation tells a compact story through physical motion and spatial relationship.
