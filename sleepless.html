sleepless in _________

Graphic Design Editorial
Graphic Design Editorial Design Typography Print Zine

sleepless in _________ is a graphic design project exploring insomnia, the quiet hours of the night, and the particular quality of consciousness that emerges when sleep will not come. The blank in the title is intentional — the experience of sleeplessness is both universal and intensely personal, located in a place that is different for everyone. The design work — editorial layout, typography, and imagery — captures the mood of 3am: restless, contemplative, strangely alive.
