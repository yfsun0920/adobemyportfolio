Pay Attention

AR Augmented Reality Interactive
Augmented Reality Interactive Mobile Spatial Design

Pay Attention is an augmented reality experience that challenges viewers to reconsider their relationship with distraction and focus in everyday environments. Through overlaid digital layers on physical spaces, the work questions how attention is directed, captured, and fragmented in contemporary life. The AR installation prompts users to look more carefully at the world around them, noticing what is typically overlooked.
