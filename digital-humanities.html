Digital Humanities Research & Web Design

Research Web Design Digital Humanities
Digital Humanities Web Design Research Information Architecture Typography

This project represents an intersection of academic research and visual communication. Digital humanities methodologies are applied to design challenges: how do we make complex data and historical narratives accessible to broad audiences through thoughtful web design? The work involves both the analytical rigor of humanities research and the craft of interface and typographic design, resulting in web experiences that communicate scholarly findings in visually engaging ways.
