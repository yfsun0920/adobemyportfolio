Cura

Motion Design Animation
Motion Design After Effects Typography in Motion Storytelling

Cura (Latin: care, attention, concern) is a motion design piece exploring themes of healing, attentiveness, and emotional care. Through kinetic typography, animated illustration, and carefully choreographed transitions, the piece creates an immersive visual experience around the concept of what it means to be cared for and to care for others. The pacing and sound design are integral to the emotional rhythm of the work.
