MoodyME

Game Design Emotional Design
Game Design Emotional Design Mental Health Illustration UX

MoodyME is a game design project centered on emotional expression and self-awareness. Players navigate a world shaped by mood — environments, music, and gameplay mechanics respond dynamically to the emotional state the player selects or discovers through interaction. The project takes seriously the notion that games can be a medium for emotional literacy, offering a gentle, introspective space to explore feelings through play.
