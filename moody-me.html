Game Design

Click here to Play

CONTROLS:

  • Click to interact
  • Choose your mood to change the world around you

ABOUT:

MoodyME is a game centered on emotional expression and self-awareness. Players navigate a world shaped by mood — environments, music, and gameplay mechanics respond dynamically to the emotional state the player selects or discovers.
